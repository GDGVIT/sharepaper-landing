Share - Paper

A small initiative by GDG-VIT to help make your work a lot easier and more organised. Share your question paper with your peers and be all time exam ready.



Awesome Features

Share all your question papers with your peers effortlessly using the Share Paper app. You can select your slot to view papers relevant to your subject. All you have to do is take a picture and you are a contributor.

  • User Interface

    Sleek, distinctive, clean User Interface.

  • Exam Prep

    Be exam ready, with all the question papers available on the go.

  • Course Papers

    Question papers sorted according to Course-ID.

  • Easy to Use

    Every minute course detail is now just one click away.

  • Spotlight

    Question papers, just one click away now.

  • Details

    All the course paper details available on the go.

  • User Friendly

    Very Helpful for VIT, Vellore Campus students.

  • Snap - Share

    Take a snap of question paper and share instantly.

App Overview

Access previous papers that have been uploaded and find all the questions on the Share Paper application. There is no need to store the hard copy of the paper for reference anymore.
